Little Ronin

Little Ronin is made of 10k unique hand drawn avatars living happily on the Polygon Blockchain.

Who is Ronin?

Ronin is hands down the best boy. Right now he only eats fruit, french fries, and muffins. He has an on-again-off-again relationship with ducks. If left alone for a second he will try to press every key on my laptop or climb something no matter how dangerous.

His favorite phrase is "yeah yeah yeah!" and his favorite word is no. Throw in Mama and Dada and that's his entire vocabulary so far. If he had his way he would just watch Cocomelon or Badanamu on Netflix all day, but we foil him with our pesky attempts at education and 1hr screen time limit.

He rather be outside than inside, and his mother unintentionally taught him to do the Naruto run. He’s sweet and sour and I love him. I created this project about him to share a little bit of him with all of you.
